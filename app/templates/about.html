Summary of HelpJM

HelpJM is a startup that will make it easy to find good services and help from your friends in Jamaica. The aim of HelpJM is to get recommendations that are provided by friends, instead of random recommendations.

Through HelpJM, you have the ability to easily contact your friends to recommend services for you. Therefore, instead of making phone calls to multiple persons when you need a good painter or a good mechanic or a good baby sitter, etc., you will now be provided with an efficient and also free way to get trusted answers from your friends.
